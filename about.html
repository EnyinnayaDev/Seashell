About Us

Welcome to S.E.A Shell Limited, an indigenous company in the maritime industry. We are dedicated to the provision of independent inspection and verification services, logistics, engineering, technical, management and commercial representation services for origanizations operating, or interested in operating in diverse business sectors. The company is wholly indigenous with 100% Nigerian shareholding.

Our commitment to excellence and sustainability drives us to deliver exceptional results in every project we undertake.

Our Mission

To work with clients to achieve safe, cost effective and efficient operations through the provision of quality professional services that meet global standards.

Our Vision

To assist clients to identify, manage and reduce risks to projects/operations and enhance safety, productivity and efficiency.

Safety Policy Statement

S.E.A Shell Ltd safety objective is to ensure strict compliance with all subsisting industry safety codes and regulations in all operations by putting in place a safety programme that ensures that adequate provisions are made for safety manuals and codes, regular drilling of our staff on safety practices, uncompromising adherence to safety rules and regulations by our personnel, regular review of our safety needs and sanctioning of personnel that breach safety rules and regulations.

Quality Assurance Policy

Our quality assurance policy is the formwork on which our operational guidelines and procedures are formulated to ensure compliance with relevant industry standards. Under our quality assurance policy, it is imperative that all tasks, jobs and assignments undertaken by the company are professionally and efficiently executed at the first instance and all processes involved in job execution and the results of the processes are properly and promptly documented and reported. Protecting the environment is a top priority of our Quality Assurance program. Standard work instructions and Job Procedure manuals are maintained for all company activities.
